Collect the Infinity Stones!

Battle Thanos and retrieve the Infinity Stones before he can decimate half of the Universe!

How to Play:

Click on a stone to increase your power. There are 6 stones to choose from, all with a Unique power that will increase your total power by a random amount. If you can successfully match your powerlevel to Thanos', You will defeat him and wrest control of the Infinity Stones from him! But be careful, if you overshoot, Thanos will win and the universe will be Decimated.
